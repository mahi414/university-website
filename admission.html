Admissions

Admissions at NIT Warangal are offered at the Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Doctoral levels.

• B.Tech admissions are through JEE (Main) followed by centralized counseling (JoSAA/CSAB).
• M.Tech/MBA/MSc admissions are based on GATE, CAT, JAM scores with counseling or institute-level process.
• PhD admissions are conducted twice a year, based on written tests/interviews and research proposals.

For more details and updates, please visit the official NIT Warangal Admission Portal.
